About Me

My name is Justinas Gibas, and I am a 28-year-old dental technician from Vilnius, Lithuania. I was born on August 8, 1995, and pursued my studies in dental technology at the Utena College Medical Faculty.

My hobbies include creating music using electronic devices and working on various computer-related projects. In my free time, you can often find me enjoying the sun and sipping water. I am proud to have trained more than a hundred people in using the latest dental software and equipment.

In the future, I aspire to develop games or create various virtual/digital spaces for different purposes. I am fluent in Lithuanian and English, and I have been actively learning programming languages to broaden my skill set.
